# West End Farmers Market

Contact

  * [\(707\) 477-8422](<tel:\(707\) 477-8422>)
  * Railroad Square \#9, Fourth Street., Santa Rosa CA 95401 



## Welcome

Located in the heart of Railroad Square, this vibrant community gathering place for local farmers, crafters and artisans to offer their wares to the public.  Every Sunday from 9:30am - 2pm, April through September. Visit the seafood guy and get some grilled oysters to enjoy in the shaded eating area before shopping for your fresh produce.
